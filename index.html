We have stepped over the verge to a new way of living

where IT is not aiding us in isolated areas, but it is omnipresent.
And as with all new inventions and gadgets, we try to fill this magical fairy dust into every thinkable place. As with everything else that moves in unchartered territory, no precedence is made yet. And our fear is that big companies and national agencies have gotten into a gold rush for our data.
The digital privacy is threatened by both commercial as well as governmental surveillance. All digital information and traffic is collected, this is why it is important to elucidate in this matter. We host a conference to enlighten students about the data collection, about their digital privacy and how they can regain control of the same.
